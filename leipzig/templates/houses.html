Houses and Kingdoms: A short explanation

Delegates are allocated to 15 Houses, with membership of a House corresponding to membership in a sub-cluster. Sub-clusters are the groups that spend the expert meeting together. Throughout the session, the Houses will complete a range of tasks targeted at creativity, incentivising exchange between participants, and making a contribution to a fun session atmosphere. Scores are updated every day of the session.

The house with most points by the end of the session wins the House Cup.
